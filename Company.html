Company Info

About Microsoft

Founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen, Microsoft has grown to become one of the world's leading technology companies, known for its software products, hardware innovations, and cloud services. The company aims to empower every person and organization on the planet to achieve more.

History

Microsoft began as a small software vendor, creating a version of the BASIC programming language for the Altair 8800. Over the years, it launched successful products such as MS-DOS, Windows, and Microsoft Office, which have become staples in both personal and professional computing.

Mission and Vision

Microsoft's mission is "to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more." The company envisions a world where technology enables everyone to succeed and thrive in their personal and professional lives.

Core Values

Microsoft is guided by a set of core values that include:

  • Integrity: Upholding ethical standards in all operations.
  • Innovation: Driving innovation to meet the needs of users.
  • Diversity and Inclusion: Fostering a diverse workforce and inclusive culture.
  • Sustainability: Committing to environmentally responsible practices.

Looking Forward

With a strong focus on cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity, Microsoft continues to innovate and adapt to the ever-changing technology landscape. The company remains committed to creating products and services that enhance productivity, creativity, and connectivity.
