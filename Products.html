Our Products

Explore the innovative software products that have defined the 1990s. From operating systems to office suites, Microsoft has provided tools that revolutionized personal and business computing.

Windows 95

The breakthrough operating system that made computing accessible for millions worldwide. Featuring an easy-to-use interface and enhanced functionality.

Microsoft Office 97

A complete office suite for productivity, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook. Empower your work with powerful tools and a familiar interface.

Internet Explorer 4.0

One of the leading web browsers of the 90s, enabling users to browse the World Wide Web with ease and security. Discover the internet like never before.

Visual Studio 97

Microsoft’s flagship development environment, providing the tools for developers to create software for Windows. Essential for professional and hobbyist developers alike.

Encarta Encyclopedia

A comprehensive digital encyclopedia with rich multimedia. An invaluable resource for students, researchers, and curious minds.
