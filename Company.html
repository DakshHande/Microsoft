About Microsoft

Founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen, Microsoft Corporation has grown from a small startup to a global leader in software, services, devices, and solutions. We strive to empower every person and organization on the planet to achieve more.

Our Mission

At Microsoft, our mission is to enable people and businesses throughout the world to realize their full potential. We deliver innovation in areas like cloud computing, AI, and productivity, making a positive impact on both large enterprises and individual users.

Global Presence

With offices, research centers, and operations in over 100 countries, Microsoft serves millions of customers globally. Our products, such as Windows, Office, and Azure, reach users in virtually every industry, helping drive success and transformation.

Innovation and Technology

From pioneering the personal computer revolution to leading advancements in artificial intelligence, Microsoft is dedicated to developing transformative technology that shapes the future of the digital world. We continue to invest heavily in research and development to fuel ongoing progress.

Corporate Social Responsibility

As part of our commitment to giving back to communities, Microsoft emphasizes corporate social responsibility, focusing on areas such as digital inclusion, environmental sustainability, and empowering nonprofits with technology solutions.
